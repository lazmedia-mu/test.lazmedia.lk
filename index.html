About Our Parish

A century-old beacon of faith, hope, and love in Kurukulawa, Sri Lanka.

St. Lazarus' Church has been a spiritual home for generations of faithful families in Kurukulawa since 1926. Nestled in a serene environment, our parish continues to nurture deep Catholic values, vibrant community life, and rich liturgical traditions. We are committed to serving God and our neighbors with joy and compassion.

Faith & Devotion

Rooted in nearly 150 years of Catholic tradition and prayer.

Vibrant Community

A welcoming parish family united in love and service.

Rich Heritage

A storied history dating back to 1868 in Kurukulawa.

Liturgical Excellence

Beautiful liturgies, choir, and sacramental celebrations.
