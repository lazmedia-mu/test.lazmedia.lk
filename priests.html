A Message from Our Priest

Dear beloved parishioners of St. Lazarus' Church,

I feel deeply blessed and honoured to have been appointed as the Parish Priest of this lovely Catholic community. From the very beginning, your warmth, vibrant faith, and deep love for the Church have been a source of great inspiration to me.

Our Parish is a true family, grounded in the Gospel and nourished by the Holy Eucharist. As we journey together in faith, my hope is that our church community continues to be a green pasture of hope, peace, and spiritual growth for everyone. I encourage each of you to actively participate in our parish life and ministries as we strive to share Christ’s love with everyone we meet on earth.

Please know that you and your families are always in my prayers. May God bless you all !

Yours In Christ,
Rev. Fr. Sisira Priyashantha
