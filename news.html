May 28, 2026 Events

Centenary Jubilee Celebrations - Preparations in Full Swing

Our parish is preparing for the historic centenary celebrations marking 100 years of faith and devotion at St. Lazarus' Church...

The entire parish community has come together to prepare for our momentous centenary celebrations, which will take place throughout the year 2026. This marks an incredible milestone of 100 years since our beloved church was founded in 1926.

Several committees have been formed to organize various events including: special events, dinner nights, community service initiatives, and a grand feast day celebration. All parishioners are encouraged to participate and contribute to make these celebrations memorable for generations to come.

Key Events Schedule:

  • Lazarine Musical Night - July 11, 2026
  • Flag-hoisting - August 20-22, 2026

Volunteers are still needed for various committees. If you would like to contribute your time and talents, please contact the parish office during working hours.

May 15, 2026 Announcement

Temporary Changes to Sunday Mass Timings

Please note that there will be temporary adjustments to our Sunday Mass schedule during the centenary preparation period...

Due to the ongoing preparations for our centenary celebrations, we need to temporarily adjust our Sunday Mass timings. This will allow our volunteers and maintenance teams sufficient time to complete the necessary preparations in the church premises.

Revised Schedule:

English Mass: 6:30 AM (instead of 7:00 AM)
Sinhala Mass: 8:00 AM (unchanged)
Tamil Mass: 10:00 AM (instead of 9:30 AM)

These changes will be in effect from June 1, 2026 until further notice. We apologize for any inconvenience caused and appreciate your understanding as we work together to create a wonderful centenary celebration for everyone.

If you have any questions, please speak to one of the ushers after mass or contact the parish office.
