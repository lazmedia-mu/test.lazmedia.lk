Centennial Celebrations

Event Schedule

Join us for a series of special events throughout our centennial jubilee year, celebrating 100 years of faith and community.

15 JAN 2026
Religious Service

Centennial Jubilee Opening Mass

Solemn High Mass to officially inaugurate our centennial celebrations, with His Eminence Cardinal Malcolm Ranjith as the main celebrant.

10:00 AM Main Church Sanctuary

Join us for this historic moment as we begin our year-long centennial celebrations. The mass will be followed by a fellowship luncheon in the parish hall. All parishioners and friends are welcome to attend this special occasion.

All parishioners welcome Fellowship luncheon included Live streaming available
28 FEB 2026
Cultural Event

Centennial Cultural Pageant

A spectacular showcase of multicultural performances celebrating our diverse parish community's rich heritage.

6:00 PM Parish Grounds

Experience the vibrant cultural diversity of our parish family through song, dance, and dramatic performances. This evening of entertainment will feature participants from all age groups within our community.

Free admission for all Food stalls available Family friendly event
17 MAR 2026
Community Gathering

St. Lazarus Feast Day Celebrations

Our annual feast day celebrations with special thanksgiving mass, procession, and community festivities.

8:00 AM - 10:00 PM Entire Parish Compound

Celebrate the feast of our patron saint, St. Lazarus, with a full day of religious and community activities including the grand procession, carnival games, and fireworks display in the evening.

Fireworks display Grand procession Carnival & games
14 DEC 2026
Religious Service

Centennial Closing Thanksgiving Mass

Thanksgiving Mass to conclude our centennial jubilee year, giving thanks for 100 years of God's blessings.

4:00 PM Main Church Sanctuary

Join us as we give thanks to Almighty God for His abundant blessings over the past century. This concluding mass will be followed by a jubilee year gratitude dinner and celebration of what we've accomplished together.

Thanksgiving dinner Year in review presentation All parishioners invited
