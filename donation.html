Join Us in Renewing Our Church

St. Lazarus' Church has stood as a beacon of faith in Kurukulawa since 1926. As we approach our centenary celebrations, we are undertaking essential renovations to preserve and enhance our sacred space for generations to come.

Your generous contributions will support critical improvements including structural repairs, interior renovations, accessibility enhancements, and modernized facilities that will serve our parish community for decades.

Renovation Projects Include:

  • Structural strengthening and repairs
  • Interior restoration and painting
  • Accessibility improvements for all parishioners
  • Modernized parish facilities
  • Centenary celebration preparations

Help Us Maintain Our Website

Running and maintaining this website incurs ongoing costs including hosting, domain registration, security updates, and technical maintenance to ensure it remains accessible and secure for all our parishioners and visitors.

Your generous donations not only support our church renovations but also help us keep this digital space running smoothly, allowing us to share our parish news, events, and spiritual resources with the community. Every contribution, no matter how small, makes a difference in helping us maintain this important connection with our parish family.

Thank you for your continued support and generosity in helping us preserve both our physical church and our digital presence for generations to come.
